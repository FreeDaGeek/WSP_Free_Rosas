Dog Walking & Pet Visits (any pet)

When you are going out of town, don't put your dogs in a kennel where it is more money and they won't even be taken good care of. Leave them in their own home so they will be comfortable and have us come over a few times a day to take them for a walk, feed them, and play with them. We will also check your mail, bring in your newspaper, water your plants, etc. We will even send you regular updates and pictures to let you know how everything is doing.
