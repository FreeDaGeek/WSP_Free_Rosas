Project Analysis

Client: Eric's Pet Service's | Project: Website Redesign | Designer: Free Rosas | Project Analysis

Site Intent

Eric's Pet Service's is currently is a site that provides all the information needed with easy. The website has links to the company facebook and images of past and current customers pets. The website sends a good message but to make it GREAT we would have to clean up the organizational structure, typographic hierarchy, image quality and overall visual design. Tweaking those would create a new refreshing look in the industy of pet services, that could attract a whole new demographic of customers.Eric's Pet Service has many local competitors. The keywords search shown that some of the local competiors have way higher rankings. Also based on Alexa.com no audiance or traffic rank was found

Target Audience

  1. Gender:Woman
  2. Age: 40- 50 years
  3. Income:Average to Upper Middle Class
  4. Marital Status:Married
  5. Homeowners:Yes

Persona #1

Michelle is 50 years old and has never been to college, married and works full time with her husband on their laundry service business the started 15 years ago. Hard work comes with easy and she is looking for a dependable pet service provider for their two family pups. Husband is open to any provider but she is a big on proffesionalism so looks for a company with excellent reviews.

Persona #2

Sara is a 43 year old, stay at home,married, mother of 2 and has attended college. Her husband works 12+ hours and she is in need to a trusts worthy pet service provider for there family dog. He kids are high school age and their family dog goes to doggy bootcamp. She feels better working with someone who has been refered to her when it comes to these matters. She worries about her husbands time consuming job and plans vacations and trips as often as she can.
